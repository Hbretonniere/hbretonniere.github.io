 During my PhD, I got many opportunities to share my works with different groups and institutions, on various conferences or workshop. I love talking about my work, with a particular attention on pedagogy. I am also involved in different journal clubs, from cosmology to Machine Learning. I also had the opportunity to give a talk to a general public to talk about galaxies. 
I am listing here some of my main presentations.

October 2021

IAP, Paris, France

12+3min

~200 

This hybrid conference (40 people attending, ~200 people online) was organsied with long general talks about the use of Machine Learning in astrophysics (mainly cosmology), short talks to present applications of those methods, and debates on the potential of Deep Learning for the community. 
I presented my two projects, the simulation of galaxies and the probabilistic deblending. I share my knowledge of the i mplications on the collaborative pipelines when Deep Learning algorithms want to be implemented. I also highlihted that Deep Learning tools start to have meaningful prediction of uncertainty.
